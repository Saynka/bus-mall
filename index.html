    Common attributes to control playback of audio
  • autoplay: boolean; use sparingly
  • controls: provides playback controls to the user
  • loop: boolean; makes it play over and over
  • muted: boolean; mutes the audio
  • src: where the sound is located
  • volume: range 0.0 to 1.0
